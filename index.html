About Us

Crafting Excellence Since 1996

Over 25 years of commitment to quality, precision, and partnership that defines every garment we produce.

Your Trusted Apparel Manufacturing Partner

EL Nasser Textile Co. is a leading apparel manufacturer based in Egypt with over 25 years of experience. With 13+ manufacturing facilities, 500+ direct employees, and 1,200+ indirect workers, we specialize in OEM, Private Label, and White Label production for international markets.

We export to North America (USA, Canada), Europe, and the Gulf Region (KSA, UAE). Our ERP-driven production and AQL-based quality control ensure every garment meets the highest standards. Currently in final approval stages for OEKO-TEX and GOTS certifications.

  • Quality Assurance

    Rigorous quality control at every production stage

  • ISO Certified Production

    ISO-certified management systems with OEKO-TEX & GOTS pending

  • Competitive Pricing

    Factory-direct rates without compromising quality
